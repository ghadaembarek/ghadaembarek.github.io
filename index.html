Ghada Embarek

I always search for human secrets. When I draw, I close my eyes and unleash my brush. I am fond of body language and express it with simple lines that are sometimes soft and sometimes harsh. I prefer painting with watercolor for its purity and sincerity of expression. Because of its one immutable action and the vast white space that the canvas posesses, my work carries freedom and ease.
- G.E.
